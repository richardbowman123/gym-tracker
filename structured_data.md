# Structured Gym Performance Database
## Richard Bowman — March 2024 to March 2026

### Weight Conventions
- **Smith machine**: Total weight reported (bar = 15kg)
- **Free bar**: Total weight reported (Olympic bar = 20kg)
- **Dumbbells**: Weight per hand (e.g. 32kg = 32kg each hand)
- **Squats / RDLs (bar)**: Total weight including bar
- **Leg press**: Plate load only (sled weight unknown)
- **Smith, free bar, and dumbbells are separate exercises** — no conversions

### Notation
- **[PB]** = personal best at time of recording
- **[PB=]** = equalling an existing PB
- **(A)** after reps = assisted (spotted) reps
- **Partial reps**: x2.5 = 2 full + 1 partial; x0.3 = barely moved
